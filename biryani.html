Biryani is a rich and flavorful Indian dish made with basmati rice, spices, and meat or vegetables. Originating from the Mughal kitchens, it is now a beloved delicacy all over India, with regional variations like Hyderabadi Biryani, Lucknowi Biryani, and Kolkata Biryani.
