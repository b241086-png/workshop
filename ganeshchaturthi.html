Ganesha Chaturthi

The Festival of Lord Ganesha

About Lord Ganesha

Ganesh Chaturthi, also known as Vinayaka Chaturthi or Vinayaka Chavithi, is a Hindu festival celebrating the birth of Lord Ganesha, the god of wisdom, prosperity, and new beginnings. It's a 10-day festival, typically falling in August or September, that involves setting up Ganesha idols, offering prayers and sweets, and concluding with the visarjan

How Ganesh Chaturthi is Celebrated

  • installing Ganesha idols in homes and public spaces
  • performing daily prayers
  • offering sweets like modaks, and culminating
  • infuses an atmosphere akin to a carnival
  • Bursting crackers
