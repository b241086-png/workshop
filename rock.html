Rock Music in India

Indian rock music blends the raw energy of Western rock with India's own cultural, regional, and linguistic diversity. From indie scenes to mainstream, it has found a passionate fanbase across generations.

Pioneered by bands like **Indus Creed**, **Parikrama**, and **Euphoria**, Indian rock took off in the 1990s and 2000s. Today, genres like alternative, metal, and fusion are thriving in cities like Shillong, Pune, and Bengaluru.
