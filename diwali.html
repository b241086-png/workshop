Diwali

The Festival of Lights

About Diwali

Diwali is a major Indian festival celebrating the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. Homes are decorated with lamps, families come together, and fireworks light up the night sky.

How Diwali is Celebrated

  • Lighting diyas and candles
  • Decorating homes with rangoli
  • Worshiping Goddess Lakshmi
  • Sharing sweets and gifts
  • Bursting crackers
