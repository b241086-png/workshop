Heritage

Introduction

India's heritage is a blend of its long history, diverse cultures, and rich traditions. The monuments, scriptures, festivals, music, and rituals reflect centuries of civilization and change.

Historical Monuments

India is home to thousands of historical sites such as forts, palaces, and ancient cities that stand as a testament to its glorious past.

Traditional Practices

Heritage also includes India's traditional knowledge systems, arts, crafts, yoga, and Ayurveda which are passed down generations.

Practices like handloom weaving, classical music, folk theatre, and oral storytelling traditions form the cultural backbone of many rural communities. Each region has its own distinct customs and legacy.

Indian heritage is also spiritual — from Vedic rituals to Bhakti movements, from Buddhist chants to Sufi poetry — reflecting the inner life of its people.
