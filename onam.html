Onam

The Festival of annual harvest

About Onam

Onam is an annual harvest festival and a major cultural event celebrated primarily in Kerala, India. It's a ten-day festival that usually falls between August and September. Onam commemorates the return of the legendary King Mahabali, and is also a celebration of the harvest season

How Onam is Celebrated

  • vibrant decorations like Pookkalam
  • traditional dances such as Thiruvathira and Kaikottikali
  • People also participate in boat races
  • promotes unity and brings families
